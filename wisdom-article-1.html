If you owned a boobies store by the end of the month you’d be a millionaire

By Nemx Cami

July 27,2025,19:45PM

Share :

Boobies Store

“Boobs run the world because everybody stares, nobody admits it, and everybody pays for it one way or another ”
Here’s the thing:
if you could bottle boobs and slap a price tag on them, you’d retire before the year’s out. Why? Because nothing pulls attention like cleavage — not politics, not religion, not even death. Humans are wired to look, crave, and pay for the curve. And in a world where attention is money, a “boobies store” would be Wall Street with nipples.

Hustle Time

Think about how insane the boob :
economy already is. Push-up bras, cosmetic surgery, lingerie, OnlyFans, strip clubs, entire industries pumping billions out of two body parts. That’s not entrepreneurship — that’s human nature on autopilot. People don’t stop to calculate ROI when lust is on the table. They just swipe, tip, subscribe, and spend. The hustle lesson here isn’t just boobs. It’s about selling the unexpected stupid thing that humans can’t resist. Rubber ducks, fidget spinners, pet rocks, potato chips shaped like Jesus — dumb ideas rake in fortunes because they tap into attention, novelty, or desire. The boobs just happen to be the undefeated champion of that field.

Some Heat

absurd desires = goldmines?
And here’s the funny, ugly truth: a “boobies store” isn’t even far-fetched. It already exists — in every mall lingerie shop, in every plastic surgeon’s office, in every webcam subscription. Society pretends it’s taboo while dumping billions into it. That’s the magic of the hustle: sell what everyone wants but nobody admits. The world runs on lust, laziness, and laughter — and boobs tick all three boxes. So yeah, open a boobies store and you’re a millionaire. But the real takeaway? Don’t chase “serious” ideas. Chase stupid, primal ones. Because humans don’t always buy what they need — they buy what jiggles.
