Swatting, Shootings, and Sextortion: The FBI Is Warning...

Sextortion Kings

“Lonely hearts are easy pickings—especially when the ‘sweet guy’ on the screen wants your wallet more than your love ”
The FBI just dropped a reality check:
your kid isn’t just texting strangers—they’re being hunted by “The Com,” a cocktail of swatting, shootings, and sextortion schemes. The predators don’t need fancy labs—they need loneliness, a bored scroll finger, and a pulse. In a world where boredom and cash collide, hustlers thrive.

Hustle Time

Here’s the ugly truth :
loneliness is a market, and your sadness has a price tag. Hustlers know this better than any MBA. They sell excitement, intimacy, and fake attention wrapped in pixels and emojis, because bored humans are desperate humans. People fall for that “lovely guy” persona and hand over credit card numbers, private photos, or real-life access, all for the thrill of feeling noticed. Life’s dull, so they buy a mirage instead of learning to move smart. And guess what? The hustlers? They’re cashing in on human boredom like it’s Bitcoin in 2010.

Some Heat

Cash is knowledge, and knowledge is survival.
But most folks are too busy crying into their Netflix to see it. The hustlers exploit that laziness and emotional hunger with surgical precision. They don’t care about your feelings—they care about your wallet and your attention. Sadness isn’t just lonely; it’s a commodity. And if you don’t wake up, the world will take your cash while giving you a false hug in return. Hustle hard, be smart , or get played—that’s the eternal law. The lonely are prey, the clever are predators, and the only thing separating you from either is whether you spend your money on skill or scams.
